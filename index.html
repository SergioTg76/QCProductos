Blockchain technology is a decentralized and distributed system that enables the secure and transparent exchange of data and digital assets between multiple parties without the need for intermediaries. Essentially, it is a public or private digital database that records all transactions in a secure and permanent manner, using cryptography to ensure the integrity and immutability of the data.

Spatial Computing

Extended Reality (XR) technologies encompass a set of tools and experiences that merge the physical and digital worlds, creating immersive environments that go beyond conventional reality. XR includes Virtual Reality (VR), which immerses the user in a completely digital environment, Augmented Reality (AR), which overlays digital objects onto the real world, and Mixed Reality (MR), which combines virtual and real elements interactively.

Spatial Computing Services:

  • Creation of mobile or web Augmented Reality applications.
  • Virtual Reality projects.
  • Development of Mixed Reality solutions.
  • XR consulting and strategy.
  • Creation of high-quality 3D content and animations.

Questiona - AI Applied to Your Business

Our artificial intelligence platform, Questiona, is designed to transform the way you access and manage information. By integrating various sources such as documents, databases, and other data sources, our AI can efficiently extract, analyze, and "memorize" large amounts of data. This advanced technology allows you to consolidate dispersed information into one place, making it easier to find precise answers without needing to navigate through multiple files.

Through an intuitive chatbot, you can directly ask questions about the data the AI has "memorized" and receive clear and quick answers in real time. No more wasting time manually searching through extensive documents—simply ask the chatbot and instantly access relevant information. This software is ideal for professionals who need to access large volumes of information quickly and accurately, optimizing time and improving decision-making.
